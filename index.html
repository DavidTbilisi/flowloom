Expressions
Standard math: + - * / ** (and ^ is treated as power). Variable t is the current time. Functions: min max exp log log10 sqrt pow abs sin cos tan floor ceil round sign, plus IF(cond, a, b) and clamp(x, lo, hi), constants PI E. A stock with no d(...) line stays constant. Aux/flow equations may reference stocks, params, and each other (no algebraic loops).
The one idea
A stock is the running integral of its net flow: stock(t+dt) = stock(t) + dt · d(stock). Write the derivative, flowloom integrates it. Reinforcing (R) loops compound; balancing (B) loops seek a goal. Limits-to-growth = an R loop meeting a B loop that tightens near a ceiling — see the logistic example.
